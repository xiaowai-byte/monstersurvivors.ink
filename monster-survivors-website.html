Game Overview

Monster Survivors is a fast-paced survival action game where you must fend off waves of monsters while collecting power-ups and upgrading your character. With each level, the monsters become more numerous and powerful. How long can you survive?

The game features procedurally generated levels, ensuring a unique experience each time you play. With dozens of weapons, abilities, and character upgrades to discover, no two runs will ever be the same.

Key Features

  • ✓ Endless survival gameplay with increasing difficulty
  • ✓ Multiple character classes with unique abilities
  • ✓ Dozens of upgradeable weapons and power-ups
  • ✓ Procedurally generated levels for endless replayability
  • ✓ Challenging boss battles that test your skills
  • ✓ Global leaderboards to compete with players worldwide

How to Play

Controls

  • • WASD or Arrow Keys: Move your character
  • • Mouse: Aim your weapons
  • • Left Mouse Button: Attack
  • • Space: Dash (if unlocked)
  • • 1-4 Keys: Use special abilities
  • • ESC: Pause game

Tips for Survival

  • • Never stop moving to avoid being surrounded
  • • Prioritize upgrades that synergize with your playstyle
  • • Collect experience orbs to level up faster
  • • Use defensive abilities when facing large groups
  • • Focus on eliminating dangerous monsters first
  • • Save special abilities for boss encounters

Monster Encyclopedia

Slimy Crawler

Slow-moving but can spawn in large numbers. Beware of their toxic trail.

Shadow Stalker

Fast and stealthy. Can disappear and reappear close to the player.

Bone Crusher

High health and damage. Best dealt with from a distance.

Toxic Spitter

Ranged monster that shoots poisonous projectiles.

Inferno Beast

Creates areas of fire that deal damage over time.

Void Titan

Boss monster with multiple attack patterns and phases.
